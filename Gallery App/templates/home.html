My Gallery App

This is the home page of my gallery app. I developed this application using django. You can add a new image from the Add New Image button. I used django's form model in the image insertion area. I also included the ck editor application in my project to enter the description text. After adding, you can click on the gallery button and see that the picture is listed on that page. When you click on the picture, you will go to the detail page. The detail page has a picture, title, description, publication date and delete button. When you click on the delete button, you will notice that the picture has been deleted from your gallery.
